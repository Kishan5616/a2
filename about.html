Our Story

100SMILES Dental Care has been providing dental services in Razole for more than thirty years. Our goal is to deliver the highest standard of oral care in a friendly, calm and professional environment.

Why is oral hygiene important?

Good oral hygiene helps prevent cavities, gum disease, bad breath and tooth loss. Brushing twice a day, flossing and visiting the dentist regularly are simple habits that protect your smile.

Because the mouth is connected to the rest of the body, poor oral health has been linked with other conditions such as heart disease and diabetes. Looking after your teeth is an important part of looking after your overall health.
