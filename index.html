Regular dental check-ups

Regular dental check-ups allow your dentist to assess your teeth, gums and mouth, helping to detect problems early when they are easier and less costly to treat.

Most people benefit from a check-up every 6-12 months, but your dentist will recommend a schedule based on your individual needs.
